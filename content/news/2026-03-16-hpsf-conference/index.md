CExA & the rest of the Kokkos teams are looking forward to meet you and hear more from you at the American Kokkos user Group at the HPSF Conference on March, the 16-20, 2026, in [Chicago](https://events.linuxfoundation.org/hpsf-conference/attend/venue-travel/).

Learn more about HPSF & Kokkos, meet Kokkos developers, share about your experience, join the Kokkos community and more!

Find out more at https://events.linuxfoundation.org/hpsf-conference/

## Location

- **Where**: Chicago, IL, USA
- **When**: March, the 16th to 20th, 2026.
- [**Agenda**: https://events.linuxfoundation.org/hpsf-conference/program/schedule-at-a-glance/](https://events.linuxfoundation.org/hpsf-conference/program/schedule-at-a-glance/)
