Every third Wednesday of the month, CExA organizes the **Kokkos tea-time**, a 45minutes time-slot to enjoy a cup of tea with a presentation around the Kokkos ecosystem (developments, usage, projects, issues, etc.) or to GPU programming at large followed discussion.
It takes place on [Zoom](https://zoom-lfx.platform.linuxfoundation.org/meeting/92397190835?password=b177af2d-9c4a-4376-9d17-b57588a88146) at 4PM Paris time, usually 7AM Pacific time, 10AM Eastern time, 11PM Tokyo time.

[Contact us](mailto:contact@cexa-project.org) to contribute a talk.
